## Working Rules

- Do not mark a milestone complete without the evidence named in the table.
- If milestone ownership changes, record the reason in `DECISIONS.md`.
- If a milestone is blocked by an external dependency, reflect that in `STATUS.md` and `BACKLOG.md`.
